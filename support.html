常见问题 · FAQ

Q：积分是什么？怎么获得？

积分是使用 AI 功能的货币。注册即送 100 积分，之后可通过应用内购买获得更多积分。

Q：What are credits and how do I get them?

Credits are used to access AI features. New users receive 100 credits upon registration. Additional credits can be purchased via In-App Purchase.

Q：积分用完了怎么办？

进入「我的」→「升级」页面，可购买积分包或订阅会员以获得更多积分。

Q：如何备份我的衣橱数据？

进入「我的」→「备份与恢复」，可将全部数据备份至本地或 iCloud。

Q：How do I backup my wardrobe data?

Go to Settings → Backup & Restore to save your data locally or to iCloud.

Q：如何切换 AI 模型？

进入「我的」→「AI 模型」，可分别切换对话模型和图像模型的供应商。

Q：购买后积分没有到账怎么办？

请稍等几分钟后重新打开 App。如仍未到账，请联系我们并提供购买截图，我们会人工核实并补发。

Q：I purchased credits but they haven't arrived. What should I do?

Please wait a few minutes and reopen the App. If credits still haven't arrived, contact us with your purchase receipt and we will resolve it manually.

Q：如何删除账号？

请发邮件至 jinjiang888666@gmail.com，注明您的注册邮箱，我们会在 3 个工作日内删除您的账号及所有相关数据。

Q：How do I delete my account?

Please email us at jinjiang888666@gmail.com with your registered email address. We will delete your account and all associated data within 3 business days.
