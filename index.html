1. Overview · 概述

ChicU ("we", "our", or "the App") is an AI-powered smart wardrobe application for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

ChicU（"我们"或"本应用"）是一款基于 AI 的 iOS 智能衣橱应用。 我们致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们收集哪些信息、如何使用这些信息，以及您对自己数据所拥有的权利。

AccountAccount Information

  • Email address (used for OTP login / 用于邮箱验证码登录)
  • Apple ID display name (when using Sign in with Apple / 使用 Apple 登录时获取)

PhotosPhotos & Images

  • Photos you choose to upload for clothing items (stored locally on your device via SwiftData + synced to your personal iCloud account)
  • Images processed by AI background removal and analysis features
  • 本应用仅处理您主动上传的服装图片，图片存储在您设备本地及您个人的 iCloud 账户中

UsageApp Usage Data

  • Credits balance and transaction ledger (stored in our backend to support billing)
  • Feature usage records (e.g., AI scoring, outfit recommendations)
  • 积分余额与消费记录（存储在后端以支持计费）

DeviceDevice & Technical Data

  • Push notification token (only if you grant notification permission)
  • Device locale and language preference
  • 推送通知 token（仅在您授权推送通知后）

PaymentPurchase Information

  • In-App Purchase transaction IDs (provided by Apple StoreKit; we do not collect card or payment details)
  • IAP 交易 ID（由 Apple StoreKit 提供；我们不收集任何支付卡信息）

3. How We Use Your Information · 信息使用方式

  • To provide core app features: wardrobe management, AI outfit analysis, style recommendations
  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session

  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • 提供核心功能：衣橱管理、AI 搭配分析、造型推荐

  • 管理积分余额及应用内购买
  • 发送推送通知（仅限您已授权）

4. AI Services & Third-Party Providers · AI 服务与第三方供应商

ChicU uses third-party AI services to power features such as clothing recognition, background removal, outfit generation, and style chat. Images and text you submit may be sent to these providers for processing:

本应用使用以下第三方 AI 服务处理图像和文本内容：

  • Google Gemini — Privacy Policy

We do not sell your images or personal data to any AI provider. Images are transmitted solely for the purpose of completing the feature you requested. 我们不会向任何 AI 供应商出售您的图片或个人数据。图片仅用于完成您所请求的功能。

5. Data Storage & Security · 数据存储与安全

  • Local device: Clothing photos, outfit data, and app preferences are stored locally using SwiftData and synced to your personal iCloud account via CloudKit.
  • Supabase backend: Account profile, credits balance, and transaction history are stored in our Supabase (PostgreSQL) database with Row-Level Security (RLS) — only you can access your own data.
  • API Keys: All AI provider API keys are stored server-side only and are never exposed to the client app.
  • 本地设备：服装图片、搭配数据及偏好设置存储在本地，并通过 iCloud/CloudKit 同步到您的个人账户。
  • 后端数据库：账号信息、积分余额及消费记录存储在受 RLS 行级安全保护的数据库中，仅您本人可访问。

我们不出售、出租或交易您的个人信息。仅在以下有限情况下共享数据：

  • With AI providers listed above, solely to process your in-app requests
  • With Apple (StoreKit) to verify In-App Purchases
  • If required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • 仅向上述 AI 供应商发送处理所需的最少数据
  • 通过 Apple StoreKit 验证应用内购买
  • 法律要求或保护合法权益时

  • Access: You can view your data within the app at any time.
  • Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us.
  • Export: You can export your wardrobe data using the built-in Backup feature.
  • Withdraw consent: You can revoke camera/photo/notification permissions at any time in iOS Settings.
  • 查看：您可随时在应用内查看您的数据。
  • 删除：您可通过联系我们删除账号及所有相关数据。
  • 导出：您可使用应用内备份功能导出您的衣橱数据。
  • 撤回授权：您可随时在 iOS 设置中撤销相机、相册、通知等权限。
